Who We Are


McKeel Development started as landscaping and general handyman work out of my Grandpa's garage in North Durham, NC.

Today, while grandpa's garage is gone, we offer quality custom builds and renovations to clients all around the Triangle.

We are committed to making the construction process as painless as possible from your first inspiration all the way to project completion.
